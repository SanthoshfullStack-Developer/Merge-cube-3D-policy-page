Privacy Policy

App: Merge Cube 3D
Platform: Android / Google Play Store
Effective date: [27-6-2026]
Last updated: [27-6-2026]

This Privacy Policy explains how Merge Cube 3D handles information when you play the game. Merge Cube 3D is a casual mobile game for Android. We do not require users to create an account, and we do not ask for personal information such as name, email address, phone number, contacts, camera, microphone, SMS, or precise location.

Information Stored Locally

Merge Cube 3D stores gameplay information locally on your device so the game can save your progress and settings. This may include:

  • Current score and high score
  • Coins and gems
  • Unlocked skins, cosmetics, and equipped items
  • Game settings such as audio, haptics, and quality preferences
  • Daily reward progress and streak information

This local gameplay data is used only to run the game experience on your device. We do not operate an account system for Merge Cube 3D, and this local save data is not used to identify you personally.

Advertising and Third-Party Services

Merge Cube 3D uses Google AdMob rewarded ads. Rewarded ads are optional advertisements that players may choose to watch in exchange for in-game rewards.

Google AdMob and related Google services may collect or process information such as device identifiers, advertising identifiers, IP address, app interactions, ad interactions, and diagnostic information. This information may be used by Google for advertising, analytics, fraud prevention, security, and service improvement.

You can learn more about how Google handles data in its services at: https://policies.google.com/privacy and about Google Mobile Ads disclosures at: https://developers.google.com/admob/android/privacy/play-data-disclosure.

How Information Is Used

Information is used to:

  • Save and restore your game progress
  • Maintain your coins, gems, unlocks, rewards, and settings
  • Provide optional rewarded advertisements
  • Improve ad delivery, security, and fraud prevention through third-party advertising services
  • Keep the game working correctly and improve the player experience

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. Merge Cube 3D does not share local gameplay save data with our own servers because the game does not use an account system or cloud save system.

Third-party advertising providers, including Google AdMob, may collect and process information as described in their own privacy policies and SDK disclosures. Their processing is governed by their policies and applicable law.

Children's Privacy

Merge Cube 3D is not intended to knowingly collect personal information from children. We do not ask children to provide personal information and do not provide an account registration system. If you believe that a child has provided personal information through the app, please contact us so we can review the request and take appropriate action.

Data Security

Local game progress is stored on your device using the app's local storage. Third-party services such as Google AdMob may transmit data using their own security practices. While no method of storage or transmission is completely secure, we aim to use standard platform features and trusted third-party services to support a safe game experience.

Data Retention and Deletion

Local gameplay data remains on your device until you delete it. You can delete local game data by clearing the app's storage in Android settings or by uninstalling the app. If a reset-save option is available inside the game, you may also use that option to remove saved progress.

Data processed by Google AdMob is handled according to Google's privacy and retention practices.

No Sensitive Device Permissions

Merge Cube 3D does not request access to your camera, microphone, contacts, SMS messages, or precise location.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
